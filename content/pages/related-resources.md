## Nobel Prizes Related to Imaging

The {{% resource_link "5eb27fcd-677c-4c87-a5c5-cf9addbd3bbe" "2009 Nobel Prize in Physics" %}} was a split award, half of it related to imaging: Willard S. Boyle and George E. Smith "for the invention of an imaging semiconductor circuit – the CCD (Charge Coupled Device) sensor."

{{% resource_link "9b4a3822-b4d3-4ddf-9f5a-0c759a4beeb9" "Imaging Life" %}} - a Nobelprize.org web site with timeline, video and articles about imaging-related innovations.
